About Me

I currently work in the healthcare sector providing care across the NHS, assisting those with mental/learning disability. I also participate in youth groups by helping individuals who are vulnerable in the community and providing guidance for people with diabetes. It has come to my attention that much of the services and support provided by the NHS are moving towards web development instead of leaflets and paper-based information. Technology plays an increasingly important part in the healthcare sector.

One of the things I would like to do is to build a bilingual website designed in an easy-read format tailored to the Somali community regarding diabetes. Diabetes is very prevalent in the Somali community, especially Type 2 diabetes which has affected several of my family members. Although there is much information available online, people tend to overlook this health issue until they and/or a relation are diagnosed with the condition. I know that a website that focuses on diabetes tailored to my target audience would help my community and general public health efforts immensely.
